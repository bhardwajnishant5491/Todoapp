## 6. TRANSACTIONS AND CONTRACTS

### 6.1 Contract Creation
- Buyers initiate contracts by submitting proposals to Farmers
- Farmers can accept, reject, or negotiate contract terms
- Acceptance creates a legally binding contract between parties

### 6.2 Payment and Escrow System

**Escrow Process:**
1. Buyer's payment is immediately debited from wallet upon contract acceptance
2. Funds are held securely in escrow (locked funds)
3. Farmer prepares and delivers the order
4. Buyer confirms receipt and quality
5. Funds are released to Farmer's wallet
6. Platform commission (4%) is deducted at release

**Locked Funds Policy:**
- Escrowed funds **CANNOT** be withdrawn
- Funds are locked until transaction completes or cancels
- In disputes, admin decision determines fund distribution

### 6.3 Delivery and Confirmation
- Farmers must deliver products as described in contract
- Buyers must inspect and confirm receipt within **48 hours** of delivery
- Failure to confirm within 48 hours results in automatic confirmation
- Quality disputes must be raised within 48 hours

### 6.4 Contract Cancellation
- Either party may cancel before farmer ships (with valid reason)
- Cancellation after shipping requires mutual agreement or admin intervention
- Refunds issued according to cancellation policy
- Repeat cancellations may result in account restrictions

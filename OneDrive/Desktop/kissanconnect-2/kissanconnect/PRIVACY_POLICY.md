## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Active Account Data

**While your account is active, we retain:**
- All account information
- Transaction history (indefinitely for legal/tax compliance)
- Communication records
- Wallet transaction logs
- Product listings history

### 6.2 After Account Closure

**When you close your account:**
- Personal information deleted within **90 days**
- Transaction records retained for **7 years** (legal requirement)
- Tax-related data retained as required by law
- Communication records retained for dispute resolution

### 6.3 Legal Obligations

We retain data longer if:
- Required by law (tax, anti-money laundering)
- Needed for ongoing disputes or legal proceedings
- Essential for legitimate business interests

### 8.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to:
- Remember your login
- Save preferences
- Track usage patterns
- Provide personalized experience

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies:**
- Session management
- Authentication
- Security features
- Platform functionality
**Cannot be disabled without affecting platform operation.**

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Google Analytics
- Usage statistics
- Performance monitoring
- User behavior tracking
**Can be disabled in browser settings.**

**Preference Cookies:**
- Language settings
- Display preferences
- Notification settings
**Optional but enhances experience.**

### 8.3 Managing Cookies

**You can control cookies through:**
- Browser settings (block all cookies)
- Private/Incognito mode
- Cookie management tools
- Opt-out of Google Analytics

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies may impair platform functionality.
